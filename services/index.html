FinViet Connect -service

Maximize your software development with our solution that offers cost-effective, direct control over quality and vast talent pool.

Reactron’s unique FinViet Connect service offshoring model combines the highly skilled and experienced software developers in Vietnam while ensuring seamless project management and communication through our Finnish-based Vietnamese team leads and project managers.

Cloud Infrastructure

Our specialty lies in AWS cloud. One proof of our dedication is that the whole team working with us is AWS certified.

Nowadays most the applications are hosted on cloud, but there are some things to remember. The architecture needs to support scaling up meanwhile remaining cost-efficient and easy to use.

Our cloud architects can support you with transferring a locally hosted application to cloud, host and secure the functionality of your cloud environment, and consult on sustainable architecture.

Let us audit your cloud.
